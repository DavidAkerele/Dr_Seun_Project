About Us

Literate was founded in 2022 and its sole focus is on enriching people new to computing with sufficient knowledge to build a career. Literate also targets people who have adequate knowledge in computing so as to help them improve on their knowledge.

ABOUT LITERATE

Literate is an online learning platform where people new to computing can come and gain valuable knowledge to help kickstart their career in computing or improve existing knowledge in specific areas.
